About

Statement · contact Updated 2026

As a Grade 12 student preparing to pursue architecture, I am drawn to the discipline because it exists at the intersection of art, culture, and human experience. To me, architecture is more than the design of buildings — it shapes how people interact, communicate, and find comfort within their surroundings.

I am especially fascinated by its ability to bridge cultures, translating history, traditions, and collective values into physical space. Whether through a centuries-old city axis in Beijing or a contemporary urban development, architecture has the power to tell stories, connect communities, and influence everyday life. As I explore undergraduate opportunities in the United States, United Kingdom, Canada, and Hong Kong, I hope to deepen my understanding of how thoughtful design can create spaces that are both meaningful and inclusive.
