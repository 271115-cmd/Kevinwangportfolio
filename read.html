AI · vision · a critical experiment Powered by Gemini

Show the machine a building and it will read it back to you — style, elements, culture — fluently and fast. The point of this tool isn't the answer. It's the blind spots: AI is trained mostly on Western, canonical, professionally-shot architecture, so it tends to force vernacular and non-Western buildings into borrowed categories. Every reading here ends by naming what it's guessing, and where it's likely wrong.
