Generative Chinese lattice · SVG/PNG Runs entirely in your browser

The lattice motifs of Chinese architecture — 冰裂 ice-ray, 回纹 key-fret, 龟背 tortoiseshell — are really just rule systems. This turns those rules into a knob you can turn: tweak, shuffle, and export a print-ready pattern. Every ice-ray seed is one of a kind.
