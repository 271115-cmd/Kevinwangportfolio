In Chinese architecture the window screen — 冰裂 ice-ray, 步步锦 step-brocade, 回纹 key-fret, 八角锦 octagon, 龟背 tortoiseshell, 斜格 trellis — turns a wall into woven light. Loom treats those rules as a living thread: weave a one-of-a-kind, seamlessly-tiling screen, dress it in a material mood, set it in a timber frame, and carry it away as a wallpaper, a print, or a seamless tile.
